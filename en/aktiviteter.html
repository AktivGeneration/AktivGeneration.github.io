Svängen LSS-supported accommodation

Date: 2024-12-07

We visited Svängen LSS accommodation to commemorate the International Day of Persons with Disabilities. The purpose of the activity was to increase empathy and understanding of children and young people for people with disabilities. By creating a meaningful day filled with community and joy, we wanted to inspire a more inclusive view of society.

During the visit, we offered the residents, who have different types of disabilities, coffee and distributed Christmas gifts to create a warm and inclusive atmosphere. We also organized activities such as music entertainment with guitar playing, games, and shared coffee.

Young people from our association actively participated to interact with the residents and thus learn the value of empathy and compassion. The day was a successful opportunity to interact with the local community and spread our association's message.
