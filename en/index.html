About Us

Who Are We?

Aktiv Generation Uppsala is a non-profit and politically independent association that promotes the development of children and youth by creating an inclusive and supportive community. We offer meeting places and activities where young people can share their experiences, develop themselves, and support younger generations. Through our various activities, we work to foster collaboration between children, mentors, and families, as well as promote social responsibility, environmental and humanitarian aid, and cultural understanding.

What We Do

Mentorship

Youth share their experiences from social, physical, mental, and emotional development with younger children.

Activities

Indoor and outdoor activities such as sports, tournaments, excursions, and picnics to promote an active and healthy lifestyle.

Social Responsibility

Participation in projects for social responsibility, environmental, and humanitarian aid in Sweden and around the world.


Cultural Understanding

Activities to promote cultural interaction and open opportunities for dialogue with different cultures in Sweden.


Press

At Aktiv Generation Uppsala, we support children and youth through a range of activities. We organize seminars, workshops, and training sessions to promote learning and experience exchange.


Our social and outdoor activities strengthen relationships between children, mentors, and families. Through projects in social responsibility, environment, and humanitarian aid, we create a positive impact in the community.
